The Ultimate Guide to Custom Mechanical Keyboards

Why Choose a Custom Mechanical Keyboard?

Finding the perfect typing experience can be difficult with standard office equipment. [span_2](start_span)A custom mechanical keyboard solves the primary problem of generic feel and poor durability[span_2](end_span). Whether you prefer the "thocky" sound of lubed switches or the "clacky" feedback of a thin aluminum plate, building your own board allows for total control over ergonomics and aesthetics.

Our community focuses on high-quality components. We explore different 75% and 65% layouts to save desk space while maintaining productivity. If you are new to the hobby, we recommend starting with a hot-swappable PCB to test different switch types without soldering.

For more professional resources on switch engineering, visit Keyboard University.
